Objectives

Care

  • Support patient care in Africa
  • Develop management guidelines for the main diseases
  • Target neurological diseases

Education

  • Assist with medical curriculum development, teaching, examinations and quality assurance
  • Prepare core lectures on neurophysiology and neurological diseases
  • Write textbook/teaching manuals on:
    • Neurology in Africa
    • Clinical examination
  • Produce teaching videos on neurological examination & disorders
  • Promote links with the following key institutions in East Africa and overseas:
    • Makere Medical School, Kampala Uganda
    • London School of Tropical Medicine
    • Centre for International Health, Bergen, Norway
  • Develop neurology services at KCMC and Tanzania
  • Promote neurological education and training in East Africa

Research

  • Carry out and publish hospital and community based studies on neurological disorders in Northern Tanzania
  • Supervise students in their research
